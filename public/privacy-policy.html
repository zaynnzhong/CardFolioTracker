Privacy Policy for Prism Portfolio

Last updated: December 10, 2025

Our Commitment: We respect your privacy. We don't sell your data, show ads, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Prism Portfolio, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication
  • Portfolio Data: Card information, prices, and transactions you manually enter
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve our service
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type for optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide secure authentication and account access
  • Store and sync your portfolio across devices
  • Send OTP verification codes for sign-in
  • Improve our service and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support when requested

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) - For secure sign-in
  • MongoDB Atlas - Encrypted database storage
  • ImageKit - Secure image storage and delivery
  • Vercel - Hosting with SSL/TLS encryption

All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

4. What We Don't Do

We do NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Share your portfolio with anyone
  • ❌ Use your data for advertising
  • ❌ Track you across other websites
  • ❌ Send spam or promotional emails

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • SendGrid: For sending OTP verification emails
  • Google Firebase: For authentication
  • MongoDB: For database storage
  • ImageKit: For image optimization and delivery
  • Google Gemini AI: For optional market insights (only when you request them)

Each service has its own privacy policy and security measures.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and browser local storage to:

  • Keep you signed in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Cache data for offline access (PWA feature)

You can clear cookies anytime through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all data
  • Export: Download your portfolio data
  • Correct: Update or correct your information
  • Opt-out: Stop using our service at any time

8. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Active accounts: As long as you use our service
  • Deleted accounts: Permanently deleted within 30 days
  • OTP codes: Automatically deleted after 5 minutes
  • Backups: Removed from backups within 90 days

9. Children's Privacy

Prism Portfolio is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (for major changes)
  • Showing an in-app notice

11. International Users

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using Prism Portfolio, you consent to this transfer.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us:

  • Email: noreply@prism-cards.com
  • Website: https://prism-cards.com

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you're a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal data is collected
  • Right to delete personal data
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination

14. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you're in the European Union, you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
